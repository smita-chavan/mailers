The Fintech Landscape
From cryptocurrencies, blockchain, and artificial intelligence to wealthtech and cybersecurity, financial technology (or fintech) has continued its meteoric rise, dominating the financial industry in 2021. In fact, the global fintech market is expected to achieve a value of approximately $324 billion by 2026, growing at a CAGR of about 23.41% between 2021 and 2026.

The term fintech refers to the computer programs and technologies that are used to support or enable banking and finance used by businesses that provide automated and improved financial services. And as it’s grown, fintech has altered consumer expectations and behavior, laying the groundwork for disruption across the financial industry and beyond it.
